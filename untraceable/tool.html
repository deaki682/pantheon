Before you post that photo…

Your photos can be traced back to your front door.

Every picture you take secretly stores the exact GPS spot it was shot — usually your home — along with the time and your device's unique ID. Post it to Marketplace, a forum, or a dating profile and anyone who saves the file can read all of it in seconds.

UnTraceable strips every trace out — right here on your device, before you share. Nothing is uploaded. There is nowhere for your photos to go.
