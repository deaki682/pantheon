Questions

Isn't Office Ally / Practice Mate free?

It is — and it's a cloud service that stores your clients' health info and is widely called clunky. This is for clinicians who'd rather their client data never leave their own computer, and who want the superbill in seconds, not a portal workflow.

Do I need an account or internet?

No account, ever. You need internet once to load the page and to buy; after that it runs offline.

Is this HIPAA-safe?

The app never transmits or stores your data anywhere — it stays in your browser — so there's no third party handling PHI. You stay in control of your own records.

Does it submit claims for me?

No — it makes the superbill document your client submits themselves. That's the whole job, done privately and fast.

A superbill is a document you complete and are responsible for — this is a formatting tool, not billing, coding advice, or a guarantee of reimbursement.
